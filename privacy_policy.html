Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect from you and how we use it.

Data Collection

We only collect information necessary for the functionality of the 3D Print Fixer service, such as file paths to your uploaded STL files.

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for the purpose of analyzing your 3D printing files and providing feedback. We do not share your data with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. Once the analysis is complete, your data is deleted.
